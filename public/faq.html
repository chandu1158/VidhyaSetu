Help & FAQ

Find quick answers to the most common questions about VidhyaSetu.

Home› Help & FAQ

🎓 For Students

How do I find a tutor? ▼
After logging into your student dashboard, go to the "Search Tutor" page. You can filter tutors by subject, location, mode (online/offline), and skill level. Click "Book Now" on any tutor card to schedule a session.
Is registration free for students? ▼
Yes! Creating a student account on VidhyaSetu is completely free. You only pay when you book a session with a tutor.
Can I cancel a booking? ▼
Yes, you can cancel a booking from your "Bookings" page as long as the session hasn't started yet. Refund policies depend on when the cancellation is made.
How do I chat with my tutor? ▼
Chat is enabled only after a booking is confirmed. Go to your "Chat" page in the student dashboard, and you'll see all your active conversations listed there.
Can I choose between online and offline sessions? ▼
Yes! During registration, you can select your preferred mode: Online, Offline, or Both. Tutors also indicate their available modes so you can filter accordingly.

👨‍🏫 For Tutors

How does the Skill Test work? ▼
After registering as a tutor, you must pass a subject-specific MCQ skill test. The test is timed, full-screen, and anti-cheat monitored. Based on your score, you'll be assigned a level: Beginner (0–40%), Intermediate (41–70%), or Advanced (71–100%).
What are the anti-cheat measures? ▼
The test detects tab switching, disables right-clicking and copy/paste, requires fullscreen mode, and auto-submits if you leave the tab or exit fullscreen. This ensures all skill ratings are genuine.
Can I retake the skill test? ▼
Yes, you can retake the test up to 3 times. After 3 failed attempts, you must wait 7 days before trying again. Your best score is recorded.
How do I get paid? ▼
Your earnings are tracked in the Earnings Dashboard. Once you accumulate a balance, you can request a withdrawal to your registered bank account or UPI ID. Payments are processed within 3–5 business days.
How do I manage my availability? ▼
In your tutor dashboard, go to "Availability & Schedule." You'll see a weekly grid where you can mark which time slots you're available. Students can only book you during your marked slots.

💰 Payments & Refunds

What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We support UPI, Debit/Credit Cards, Net Banking, and Wallets through our integrated payment gateway (Razorpay). International payments are also supported via Stripe.
Is my payment information secure? ▼
Absolutely. We never store your card details. All payments are processed through PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways with end-to-end encryption.
When will I receive a refund? ▼
Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method. Contact support at support@vidhyasetu.in if your refund is delayed.

🛠️ Technical Support

I forgot my password. What should I do? ▼
On the login page, click "Forgot Password?" and enter your registered email. You'll receive a password reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.
The chat is not working. What should I do? ▼
Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. Make sure you have a confirmed booking with the person you're trying to chat with, as chat is only unlocked post-booking. Contact support if the issue persists.
Can I use VidhyaSetu on my mobile phone? ▼
Yes! VidhyaSetu is fully responsive and works on all modern browsers on mobile, tablet, and desktop. Note: The Skill Test requires a desktop/laptop with a large screen for fullscreen mode.
