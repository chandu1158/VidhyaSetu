Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Home› Privacy Policy
📅 Last Updated: March 2025

Welcome to VidhyaSetu. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when registering or using our services:

  • Full name, email address, phone number
  • College name, location, subjects of interest (Students)
  • Occupation, experience, teaching subjects (Tutors)
  • Profile information and availability schedules
  • Booking history, payment details, and chat messages
  • Skill test results and assigned skill levels (Tutors)

We also automatically collect certain technical data: IP addresses, browser type, device information, and usage patterns through cookies and similar technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To facilitate bookings between students and tutors
  • To process payments and maintain transaction records
  • To enable chat communication between matched pairs
  • To verify tutor skill levels through our assessment system
  • To send service updates, notifications, and support messages
  • To improve our platform's features and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Between Students and Tutors: Profile information is shared to facilitate bookings and sessions
  • Payment Processors: Payment data is shared with Razorpay/Stripe for transaction processing
  • Firebase (Google): Our database and authentication services are powered by Google Firebase
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect the rights of VidhyaSetu users

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Firebase Authentication for secure login
  • Firestore security rules for role-based data access
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways
  • Regular security audits and updates

5. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect some platform features.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored by VidhyaSetu
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Data portability (receive your data in a structured format)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@vidhyasetu.in.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law to retain it longer.

8. Children's Privacy

VidhyaSetu is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has registered on our platform, contact us immediately at support@vidhyasetu.in.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email or in-platform notification. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us
