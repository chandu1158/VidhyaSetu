Our Mission

VidhyaSetu was born from a simple belief: every student deserves access to a great tutor, and every talented educator deserves a platform to share their knowledge and earn fairly.

We built a platform where tutors are skill-verified through rigorous tests, students can find the perfect match, and the entire process — from booking to payment — is seamless and transparent.
